Everything You Need

From cosy rooms to bonfire nights — here is what makes your stay truly special

Facilities

What's Included

Everything is designed to let you relax, explore, and feel completely at home

🍽️

Home-Cooked Meals

Experience the joy of authentic mountain cuisine — fresh, seasonal, and made with love using vegetables grown in our own kitchen garden.

  • Breakfast included with every room
  • Lunch & dinner available on request
  • Vegetarian & non-vegetarian options
  • Special dietary needs accommodated
☕

Tea & Snacks Station

Help yourself to hot chai, coffee, and light snacks throughout the day at our open common area — a favourite gathering spot for guests.

  • Complimentary tea & coffee all day
  • Evening snacks with local flavours
  • Kettle in every room
📶

Free High-Speed WiFi

Stay connected with our reliable WiFi available across the property — so you can work remotely or stay in touch with loved ones.

  • Available in all rooms & common areas
  • Password shared at check-in
  • Stable connection for video calls
🚗

Parking & Transport

Ample free parking on-site. We can also help arrange local transport, taxis, and guided excursions upon request.

  • Free on-site parking for all guests
  • Airport / station pickup available
  • Local taxi assistance
🔥

Evening Bonfire

Gather around a crackling bonfire under a sky full of stars. Our evening bonfires are where the best conversations and memories are made.

  • Seasonal bonfire evenings
  • Stargazing on clear nights
  • Snacks and stories around the fire
🌿

Garden & Outdoor Space

Wander through our flower gardens, sit in the sun with a book, or simply breathe the fresh mountain air in our beautiful outdoor spaces.

  • Landscaped kitchen & flower garden
  • Outdoor seating & hammock area
  • Children's play space
🛁

Hot Water & Bathrooms

Every room has an attached, clean bathroom with reliable hot water — essential for those crisp mountain mornings and evenings.

  • 24-hour hot water supply
  • Towels & toiletries provided
  • Daily housekeeping included
❄️

Room Heating

Stay warm on cool mountain evenings with room heaters available in all rooms — so you can snuggle up comfortably year-round.

  • Room heaters in all rooms
  • Woolen blankets & extra quilts
  • Warm common lounge area
📸

Photography Spots

Our property is a photographer's paradise — from sunrise vantage points to flower-framed doorways and misty valley views.

  • Sunrise viewpoint access
  • Garden photo corners
  • Photography assistance by hosts
