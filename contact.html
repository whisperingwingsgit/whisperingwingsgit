Getting Here

How to Reach Us

🚂

By Train

Nearest major railway station: New Jalpaiguri (NJP) — approximately 149 km from Padamchen by road. Private taxis and shared cabs are available from NJP / Siliguri for the Silk Route circuit.

✈️

By Air

Nearest airport: Bagdogra Airport (IXB) — commonly used by visitors traveling to East Sikkim. From Bagdogra, taxis can be hired onward to Padamchen via Rongli.

🚗

By Road

Well-connected by road from Siliguri — approximately 104 km away. Usual route is via Kalimpong / Pedong / Rongli. Private vehicles and reserved cabs are the most convenient options.

FAQs

Is breakfast included in the room rate? +

Yes! Complimentary breakfast is included with all room bookings. Lunch and dinner are available at additional cost and must be requested in advance.

The homestay is beautiful year-round. [Season 1] offers [description]. [Season 2] is ideal for [description]. We recommend avoiding [heavy monsoon period] for trekking.

Can we accommodate special dietary needs? +

Absolutely. Please inform us of any dietary requirements when booking and our hosts will make sure every meal is prepared accordingly.

Is the property suitable for children and elderly guests? +

Yes, very much so. We love having families. We can provide cribs, extra blankets, ground-floor rooms for elderly guests, and gentler activities for all age groups.

What is your cancellation policy? +

We understand plans change. Cancellations made 7+ days in advance receive a full refund. Within 7 days, we retain 50% of the booking amount. Please contact us directly for special circumstances — we are always flexible and understanding.
