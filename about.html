Our Story

A family, a dream, and a valley full of stories waiting to be shared

Who We Are

The Whispering Wings Family

Nestled in the heart of the hills for generations, our family has always lived in rhythm with the mountains. We grew our own food, foraged from the forests, and welcomed every traveller who knocked on our door with a warm cup of tea.

In 2025, we opened Whispering Wings Homestay officially — turning our ancestral home into a place where guests from across India and the world could experience the simple magic of mountain life. What we offer is not a hotel experience; it is a living, breathing home full of stories, laughter, and the scent of real cooking.

Today, we run the homestay with help from our children, ensuring that every guest receives the same warmth and personal attention that has made us a beloved destination for repeat visitors.

What We Believe

Our Values

These principles guide everything we do — from how we greet you to how we cook your breakfast

🤲

Genuine Hospitality

We treat every guest like a long-lost cousin coming to visit. No formal check-in scripts — just a warm welcome, a cup of tea, and a genuine smile that lasts your whole stay.

🌱

Sustainable Living

Our kitchen garden grows the herbs and vegetables that end up on your plate. We compost, conserve water, and try to leave as light a footprint as possible on this beautiful land.

🍲

Real, Home-Cooked Food

Every meal is prepared fresh using local produce and age-old family recipes. No frozen ingredients, no packet masalas — just honest, delicious home cooking.

🏔️

Love for This Land

We share our knowledge of the hills, trails, local culture, and seasonal rhythms — helping guests connect deeply with the place they are visiting, not just pass through it.

👶

Family-Friendly Always

Children are extra special guests here. We have space, activities, and stories for little travellers — because some of the best memories are made on family getaways.

🔐

Safety & Comfort

We take safety seriously — clean linens, hygienic kitchens, reliable hot water, and attentive hosts who are always a call away, day or night.
